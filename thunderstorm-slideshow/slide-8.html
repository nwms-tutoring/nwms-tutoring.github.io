We know all about thunderstorms now,
but when will the next one happen?


To know when the next thunderstorm will happen, you should combine official forecasting tools with personal observation of weather clues. The most reliable methods involve using official weather alerts and dedicated apps/websites, as professional meteorologists use advanced technology to track and predict storms.
Some easy ways to do that is to just look at what is happening around you. Do you hear rumbling in the sky? Or do you see clouds and/or lightning? That is the most reliable way to determine if a thunderstorm is approaching.
