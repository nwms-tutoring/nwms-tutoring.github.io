Thunderstorms can't be stopped, but how can we reduce the damage?


To reduce thunderstorm damage, secure your home by strengthening its structure, protecting windows and doors, and maintaining drainage systems. You can also protect your property and belongings by securing outdoor items, trimming trees, and installing surge protectors for electronics.
(Surge protectors safeguard electronics from damaging voltage spikes by diverting excess power to the ground.)
